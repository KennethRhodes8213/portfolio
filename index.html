What I Do

Creating a website yourself is hard, you can use an interface to drag and drop items, but you're just going to be like everyone else. That's where I come in, I can make you a unique website that sets you apart from your competition and makes you stick out in this sea of copycats.

The Process

  1.  Your email is sent! Now it's time to wait 24-48 hours for a response, get comfortable especially if my schedule is congested...
  2.  Planning You've been accepted! Now it's time we swap ideas and figure out a wireframe. This could take a few hours to complete!
  3.  Rough Draft In the period of 24 hours you will recieve your first rough draft of your website, this will be an unfinished prototype to see if you like it, if you do we'll keep going, if not we'll go back to step 2.
  4.  The Finishing Touches You liked it! Now it's time to add that last bit of spice to your site. This could take anywhere between 24 to 48 hours to complete, hang tight!
  5.  Pay Day! I know, I know, it's sad to let that money go, but it's for a good cause, it takes money to make money. If you liked the site, you should recieve your invoice, if you didn't, then we'll start making edits together!
  6.  Setup and Finish It's either time to publish your site or say farewell. If you think you can do it on your own or don't want to pay the extra few bucks, then hopefully we'll talk again soon! Otherwise, let's keep going, let's hook up these files to a domain, setup Cloudflare® and deploy!
